Our History

Founded in 2003, Larrys Landscaping began as a small, family-owned business in Belmont, California, with a mission to provide exceptional landscaping services to local homeowners and businesses. Over the years, the company built a reputation for quality, reliability, and personalized care, transforming residential and commercial properties with custom designs, maintenance, and hardscaping solutions. With a commitment to sustainable practices and an eye for detail, Larry Landscaping has grown into a trusted name in the community, known for creating beautiful, functional outdoor spaces that enhance curb appeal and value.

Our Mission

Our mission at Larrys Landscaping is to enhance the beauty and functionality of outdoor spaces through innovative, sustainable landscaping solutions, while delivering exceptional service and value to every client.

Our Values

At Larrys Landscaping, we are guided by a set of core values that shape everything we do. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our attention to detail, craftsmanship, and dedication to creating landscapes that are both beautiful and functional. Above all, we are passionate about building lasting relationships and delivering exceptional results that exceed expectations. Your outdoor space is in good hands with us.
